Events

Always something to do!

You wouldn't realize it, but there are a number of things and events that are held to celebrate Halloween pretty much everywhere you can think of. While mentioned trick-or-treating previously, this list will encompass not just trick-or treating but also other events you may not have been aware of.

  • Trick-or-treating

    Trick-or-treating is probably the most widely-known way to celebrate Halloween. It can be done at any time of day on Halloween, but if you wish to enhance your experience, you can do it when it is darker to make it extra creepy. You can go around from house to house, knocking on the doors and saying "trick or treat!" This will promptly reward you with some candy if you have a costume on. The goal is simply to get as much the process to get more candy and have more fun, you should consider trick-or-treating with a group of friends.

  • Haunted houses and attractions

    Sometimes, in either public areas or in ammusement parks, you can find attractions and haunted houses that may require fees for admission but overall can provide great fun. Once again, similar to trick-or-treating, many of these events are better experienced at night and or with friends because of the nature of Halloween. In either case, haunted houses typically consist of tight corridors, random jumpscares, and creepy images or sounds that is intented to rattle you with fear. This applies to other attractions as well, however none of them will be for the faint of heart. You should go in, if you dare!

  • Game Events

    For those either stuck at home or suffer from being shy and anti-social, you also have a way of celebrating Halloween that doesn't require any type of physical or social interaction. At this time of year, for almost the whole month in some cases, many popular games will feature a Halloween themed event that can make the mood feel right for the time of year. These events will typically add new things to the game, such as halloween-related rewards, quests, monsters, pets, mounts, etc. Halloween events don't happen in every game, however when they do and you have a group of friends that want to play too, you can have just a good of a time playing your game as actually going outside.

  • Halloween parties

    Sometimes, people like to celebrate Halloween by throwing a halloween themed party and having fun with loved ones and friends. These parties often times feature music, people in costume, cupcakes, snacks, and if the party is really cool, a smoke machine. This doesn't just pertain to individuals either, as sometimes there are some public places such as schools and certain stores that like to hold their own parties and invite people to them.
