Thriller

Whenever anybody thinks of music and Halloween at the same time, the song that normally everyone thinks about is Thriller by Michael Jackoson. Even though the song released in 2009, today it is still seen in ads like Party City and shown throughout schools and parties or events. Thats why even 11 years after it's original release, new generations still know and also remember Thriller, arguably making it the most popular Halloween song to date.
